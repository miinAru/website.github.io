  1. Running man

    2. WATCHIG ONLINE

    Running Man is a Korean TV show; part of the Good Sunday block on SBS. It premiered on July 11, 2010, and is currently the longest-running program on SBS.
    The show is shot in the genre of "chase", a genre of different shows in an urban environment. Presenters and guests participate in survival missions to win the race.She attracted attention with her cast: Yoo Jae-Seok and Kim Jong-kook host this show after leaving Family Outing; comedian HaHa, who has a lot of experience and success on a very successful and long-lived program as "Endless Challenge"; Gary, a member of the popular hip-hop duo "Leessang" (left the show after 324 episodes to focus on a singing career, was a guest in 325 episodes); Lee Kwang-soo, actor, a newcomer to entertainment shows; Song Ji-Hye, actress, was the only girl in the cast until episode 346, came in episode 2 and became a regular member in episode 6; Ji Seok Jin, TV host, the oldest member of the program; Song Joon Ki, actor, left the show after the 41st episode. Lizzie, the singer, came in episode 13 and left in episode 25. Each episode is interesting in its own way, and sometimes you don't know what the director and producers of the show will come up with this time. Comedian Yang Se Chan and actress Jung So Min joined the main cast of the show on April 3, 2017 (episode 346 aired on April 17, 2017).

  2. Mr.Queen

    3. WATCHIG ONLINE

    In our time, Jang Bong-hwan works as a chef in the Blue House.
    Suddenly, he finds himself in the Joseon era. But this is not the biggest problem. Worst of all, his soul is now trapped in the body of Queen Chorin (Shin Hye Sun). What should he do? It seems that it's time to remember a couple of historical dramas to figure out who is who at the royal court and learn how to survive among the court intrigues because the queen is full of detractors. Can he handle it? Or...is it more correct to say she now?
    King Chol Jung (Kim Jong Hyun) looks like a puppet in the hands of those who really rule the country. But in fact, he hides quite strong traits of his character.
    Queen Sungwon (Pae Jung Ok) is the wife of the late King Sanjo. It is she who holds the true power in the country.
    Kim Jwa-Geun (Kim Tae - woo) is the younger brother of Queen Sungwon. He is very ambitious and dreams of power.

Mouse

WATCHING ONLINE

A fascinating story set in a world where people can identify psychopaths using a fetal DNA test in the womb.
Jung Ba Rym is an honest and kind rookie cop who believes injustice. After encountering a psychopathic serial killer whose ruthless murders have horrified the entire country, his life changes completely.
Guo Mu Chi is an experienced detective whose parents were killed when he was young. Since then, the goal of his life is to find the killer and avenge the death of his parents. To catch criminals, Guo Mu Chi is ready to resort to any means, without caring about the law or the rules.
Oh Bon Yi is a troubled high school student who lives with her grandmother and is well versed in martial arts. Every time she runs into her neighbor, police officer Jung Ba Rym, they start having fun bickering with each other.
Choi Hong Joo is a talented director-producer, known as "Sherlock Hong Joo". She also keeps a secret that linked her to the killer as a child.

Penthouse (1,2)

WATCHING ONLINE

The Hera Palace is a luxury skyscraper in the center of Gangnam. Its inhabitants are fabulously rich, influential, and ambitious. They are capable of doing anything to achieve their goals and satisfy their ambitions.

The Uncanny Counter

WATCHING ONLINE

Each of the evil spirit hunters from the group called "Counter" has its own special superpowers. The group is working undercover at a noodle restaurant.
Seo Moon (Cho Pyong Gyu) is a high school student and the youngest in the group. As a child, he was involved in a suspicious car accident. His parents were killed in an accident, and he, after several operations, limps on his left leg.
Ga Mo Tak (Yoo Joon San) is a former police officer. 7 years ago, he was in an accident, because of which he lost his memory. His superpower is physical strength. He's Seo Moon's mentor.
Do Ha-Na (Kim Se-Jung) can sense evil spirits hundreds of miles away thanks to his super hearing. When she touches someone, she can read their memories.
Joo Ma Ok (Yom Hyo Ran) is the chef of a noodle restaurant. She has healing abilities and is emotional support for others in Counter.
Choi Jang-mol (Ahn Seok-hwan) is the oldest spirit hunter in Korea, a "living legend" who is responsible for managing the group's expenses.

Genre Created by Starring Year
comedy, mystery, thriller Yoo Seong-dong, Kim Se-beom, Yoo Seong-dong, Ye Ji-na Yoo Joon-san, Ahn Seok-hwan, Yom Hyo-ran, Kim Se-Jung, Cho Pyong-gyu 2020-2021

Vincenzo

WATCHING ONLINE

Vincenzo Casano (Song Joon Ki) grew up in Italy and became a cold-blooded strategist and mafia lawyer. He looks like a nice person, but he can take revenge without hesitation. He also has excellent negotiation skills. Vincenzo returns to South Korea after the outbreak of the mafia war.
Hong Cha Yong (Jung Ye Bin) is the best lawyer in South Korea and works for a large law firm, Usan. She's the kind of lawyer who would do anything to win a case.
Jang Joon-woo (Ok Taek-Yong) is a trainee lawyer who works in Wusang with Hong Cha-yong. He grew up abroad and has a beautiful appearance and an innocent smile. He trusts and admires his teacher Hong Cha Yong.
